Fighting misinformation

TruthLens is a web-based application that uses Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to detect whether a news article is real or fake. The system analyzes news content using Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques and a trained machine learning model.

It also integrates real-world news data from trusted APIs to make the system practical and usable in everyday scenarios.
